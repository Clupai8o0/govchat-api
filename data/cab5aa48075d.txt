Information Publication Scheme (IPS)












On this page:






What is the IPS
The Information Publication Scheme (IPS) is established under the Freedom of Information Act 1982 (FOI Act). Under the IPS, agencies are required to:
publish an
Agency IPS Plan
publish specific categories of information (set out below)
consider proactively publishing other government information.
More information about the IPS can be found on the
Office of the Australian Information Commissioner
website.
Required information
Agency IPS Plan
The department’s
IPS Plan
sets out how the department meets its IPS obligations.
What we do
Information about what we do and our strategic priorities can be found on the
About Us
page.
The
Administrative Arrangements Order
also specifies the subject matters dealt with, and the legislation administered by, the department.
Who we are
Information about our organisation and structure, as well as statutory appointments can be found on the following pages:
Our Ministers
Our executive
Our organisational chart
Details of our statutory appointments
Information about careers with us
Our reports and responses to Parliament
Our information tabled in Parliament, or routinely provided to Parliament includes:
Annual reports
Responses to senate orders
Details about agency advertising and public information projects
Routinely requested information and FOI disclosure log
The
FOI disclosure log
lists information that we have released in response to an FOI access request, subject to certain exceptions.
Public reviews and consultations
Details of our policy proposal consultation arrangements can be found on our
public reviews and consultations
page.
Operational information
Operational information is information we hold to assist us to perform or exercise our functions or powers in making decisions or recommendations affecting members of the public. This includes our plans, policies and guidelines relating to those decisions and recommendations.
Our operational information can be found throughout our website, alongside related subject matter or by using the
search
function on our website.
Other information
Our priorities
We publish the details of our current and past
corporate plans
and
annual reports
.
Our finances
We publish information about our finances, including information relating to our budget, pay and grading structures in the agency, tendering and contracts.
Information about our
budget
Details of our major contracts are listed on the
Australian Government’s AusTender site
Details of our grant opportunities and grants awarded are listed on the
Australian Government’s GrantConnect site
Information about our pay and grading structures is available in our
enterprise agreement and pay determinations
Our lists and datasets
We publish information about our lists and datasets including:
Early childhood data and reports
Australian Government
funding of schools data
Higher education statistics
Other datasets are available at
data.gov.au
.
Our submissions
We often make submissions on education issues raised by inquiries of the Senate Standing Committees on Education and Employment which can be found on the
inquiries website
.
Contact us
You can contact us about access to information or documents under the FOI Act by sending an email to
FOI@education.gov.au
.
If you have a question or would like to give feedback on any aspect of the IPS or the information published, you can also contact us online, by email, phone or by mail with the details on our
contact us
page.
