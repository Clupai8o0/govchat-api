Annual Reports










The Annual Report provides information about the Department's achievements, corporate arrangements and performance.



On this page:






2023–2024 Annual Report
The
Department of Education 2023–24 Annual Report
was tabled in Parliament on Monday 28 October 2024.
The Annual Report includes the department’s audited financial statements and audited annual performance statements for the year ending 30 June 2024. The report provides information about the department’s achievements, corporate arrangements and performance.
Past annual reports
are available for the former iterations of our department on our website.







Department of Education 2023-24 Annual Report





Created:

28 October 2024





The Department of Education 2023–24 Annual Report has been prepared in accordance with section 46 of the
Public Governance, Performance and Accountability Act 2013
(PGPA Act) and the Department of Finance’s Resource Management Guide No.135 – Annual reports for non-corporate Commonwealth entities.
