Public reviews and consultations










The Department of Education and the agencies and boards within the portfolio, often request public comment on a range of topics or programs.




This page provides information on submission rounds, consultations and reviews undertaken in the Education space. The views expressed in these submissions are the views of the respective author(s) and does not necessarily reflect the views of the department. The department makes no representation or warranty about the accuracy, reliability, currency or completeness of any third party information.
For more information, please read the
Terms and Conditions for public submissions
.
If you are having trouble accessing the information contained within these public submissions, or are trying to locate an archived submission round that is not listed below, please
contact the department
.
